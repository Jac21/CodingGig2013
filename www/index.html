Coding Gig 2013

Aggressive terms by region in our sample tweets

The culprit of the Boston Bombings tweeted messages hinting he was going to attempt something drastic the days following up to the event. Our team had the idea that one could track potential, or currently occuring crimes by tracking keywords in people's Twitter tweets. We implemented this idea using the tweets' latitude and longitude, compared it to a map of crime density in the U.S., and analyzed the data to find out if there was a correlation between the threatening keywords and areas with higher crime rates.
